⚡ WHAT IS DINOCRAFT?

🌍

EXPLORE A BIG WORLD

A huge procedurally generated world with caves, biomes, ores, and hidden surprises.

⛏️

MINE & CRAFT

Dig up coal, iron, gold, and diamonds. Craft planks, torches, and chests.

🦴

DINO SECRETS

Unearth fossils and find glowing dino eggs hidden in trees and deep underground.

🌙

DAY & NIGHT

A full day/night cycle. Place torches to light up caves or it gets very dark very fast.

📱

MOBILE FRIENDLY

On-screen controls so you can play on your phone or tablet too.

🎮

NO DOWNLOAD

Runs right in your browser. No install, no account. Just click and play.

👾 ABOUT THIS GAME

Dinocraft is an original 2D browser game built from scratch using HTML5 Canvas and vanilla JavaScript. Inspired by classic block-building games, it adds a prehistoric twist — fossils, dino eggs, and a pixelated dinosaur hero.
